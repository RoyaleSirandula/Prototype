WHAT EXACTLY IS SCHOLAR'S NEST?
Scholar’s Nest™ is located on Nyali Golf Drive, Nyali Estate, Mombasa, Kenya. It is a refreshing student living community by Nest™, a visionary real estate firm redefining modern accommodation. Designed for comfort, affordability, and focus, it blends fully serviced rooms with study-friendly space and vibrant social zones that foster belonging.
From study lounges, fast internet speeds, green spaces, in-house chefs, and serene pools to 24/7 service, top-tier security, and collaborations with leading schools, every detail supports academic excellence and personal growth. More than housing, Scholar’s Nest™ is a lifestyle hub where students thrive, connect, and create their best stories.
