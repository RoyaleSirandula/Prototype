Sights & Sounds -
Karibu Nyumbani
Scholar’s Nest™ by Nest™ provides an inspiring student housing experience that combines modern comfort, safety, and community spirit. Our thoughtfully designed spaces feature beautifully staged interiors, panoramic views, and carefully curated amenities that make it easy for students to focus, relax, and connect. We foster an environment where every detail—from natural light to acoustics—supports well-being and academic success. At Scholar’s Nest™, students find more than just accommodation: they find a vibrant community that supports personal growth, lifelong friendships, and memorable experiences. Life here is anything but ordinary. It’s a lifestyle crafted for the modern student — where elegance meets purpose. Every room, hallway, and shared space is designed to inspire focus and connection, helping you live and learn effortlessly in rhythm with success.

OUR PARTNERS

Organisations, Sustainability Partners & Institutions Supporting Nest

Scholar's Nest collaborates with leading schools, universities, organisations, and sustainability partners to provide unrivalled service and support for our students.

Be a Nest™ partner

  • Braeburn International School, Mombasa

    Leading international school with strong academic and holistic programmes.

  • Light International School, Mombasa

    Prestigious international school in Nyali supporting global student excellence.

  • Aga Khan Academy, Mombasa

    World-class institution emphasising ethical leadership and community service.

  • Mombasa Academy

    High-quality private school in Nyali, with British curriculum and holistic focus.

  • Jaffery Academy, Mombasa

    International school providing well-rounded education and service programmes.

  • Clean Up Kenya

    Empowering children through beach cleanups, environmental education and youth stewardship programmes.

  • Green Action Project (Be The You Foundation)

    Planting fruit tree seedlings in schools and cleaning shores along the Indian Ocean.

  • Omeriye Foundation

    Conducts Nyali beach cleanups and engages youth in environmental awareness acts.
