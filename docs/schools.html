For Schools and Educators

Inspiring student growth through safe, immersive experiences

Scholar’s Nest offers structured residential programs that extend learning beyond the classroom. Each program includes clear safety protocols, supervised activities, and tailored learning modules that support curriculum goals.

We work with teachers to design trips that focus on cultural exchange, leadership, and practical skills. These trips include local museum visits, community service projects, and experiential workshops led by local experts.

  • Safety First with 24/7 staff, secure access and private dorm options for school groups.
  • Custom Learning Modules designed with partner educators to reinforce class topics and learning objectives.
Teachers can request custom itineraries, risk assessments and pre-visit orientations for students and chaperones.

University Pathways and Global Opportunities

Beyond graduation, towards the world

For senior residents, the Global Pathways Program offers targeted support for admissions to universities in the United States, Canada, Japan, Europe and beyond.

  • In-house Education Consultants who guide residents through essays, scholarships and selection.
  • Global Alumni Mentorship pairing aspiring students with former Nest residents studying abroad.
  • Exam Readiness with SAT, IELTS and other test prep sessions on site.
  • Pathways Fair a signature annual event with university representatives and scholarship advisors.

What to expect in your application process

  • Personal statement coaching and iterative feedback.
  • Portfolio review for arts and design applicants.
  • Mock interviews and scholarship strategy sessions.
  • Visa orientation and pre-departure briefings.

Community and NPOs

Making local impact through shared action

Scholar’s Nest invites community organisations and sustainability groups to design and lead projects with residents. Our role is to provide venue, visibility and logistical support so projects can scale and sustain.

We welcome groups to lead beach cleanups, guided park hikes, yoga and reflection sessions, and nature trails that combine conservation with wellbeing.

Typical activities we host

  • Beach cleanups and coastal restoration projects.
  • Park hikes and guided nature trails with local ecologists.
  • Yoga mornings, mindfulness sessions and reflection circles.
  • Community fairs, craft markets and sustainability workshops.

Partnership impact

Projects run with us aim to create measurable outcomes, such as reduced single-use waste, increased native tree cover, and greater public awareness of local biodiversity. We publish short reports after major activities so partners and residents can see progress.

What we are looking for

Partners who share values and practical impact
